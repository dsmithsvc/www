Steve Beach

Steve works from his home office in the heart of Silicon Valley. He has been working in the EDA industry since 1997.

Steve received a B.S. degree in agricultural biology from California Polytechnic University Pomona in 1983. He has worked as a manager in the agricultural industry for many years.

In 1997, Steve chose to make a career move and capitalize on the growing electronic industry in Silicon Valley. He has found a niche as an engineer in PCB layout and has worked with Dan and Mark as a fellow team member since that time.
