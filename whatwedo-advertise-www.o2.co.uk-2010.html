PCB Layout

Our main focus is to provide quality PCB layout services to companies in Silicon Valley. We pride ourselves in doing a good job. Our customers appreciate that we take time to learn about the product and understand each circuit in the schematics. With this knowledge we proceed to create a very orderly and logical layout. A circuit board can only be good as it's schematics and design. With netbooks and laptops decreasing in price and mobile phone deals being offered for iPhones, Blackberrys, and other smartphones, the increase of PCB's is evident. We will work with you to come up with a customizable and effective layout.

Schematic Capture

We also provide schematic capture services using many of the most popular software tools. A big advantage in having us create your schematics is that we will create each symbol with accurate attributes prepared for layout.
