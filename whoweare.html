Dan Smith

Dan works from his home office in the beautiful Rocky Mountains. He has been working in the EDA industry since 1989.

Dan received a B.A. degree in education with a teaching credential from San Jose State University in 1980. He taught Spanish at the high school level for eight years and then made a career move into the electronic industry. He worked for Silicon Valley Consultants for many years and then had the opportunity to purchase the company. The company did well as the economy flourished throughout the 1990s.

In 2000, Hytek Services purchaed Silicon Valley Consultants. Dan worked for Hytek Services as an engineering manager overseeing a team of engineers in hardware design.

In 2003, Dan left Hytek Services and set up an office in his home. He enjoys the opportunity to be home with his family and he enjoys the benefits of being self employed.
